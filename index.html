About Us

Gramzz is a health-focused food delivery service based in Hyderabad, India. We provide a variety of products aimed at meeting daily nutritional needs, including sliced fruits, dry fruits, salads, and millet-based products. Our goal is to make healthy eating convenient, accessible, and fun!

Our Product Offerings

Fresh Fruits

Seasonal, sliced fruits, freshly sourced to provide the right balance of vitamins, minerals, and fiber.

Healthy Salads

Ready-to-eat salads composed of fresh vegetables, fruits, and additional healthy components like nuts or seeds.

Dry Fruits

Almonds, Pistachios, and more to offer your daily dose of nutrients.

Millet-Based Meals

Pre-prepared millet-based batters rich in protein, fiber, calcium, and magnesium, helping in weight management and improving digestion.
